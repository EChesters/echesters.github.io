SuperBreak

SuperBreak is a travel website for short breaks. I moderated a day of usability sessions with 6 participants, across desktop and tablet, discovering general issues and areas for AB testing of their site. From this, I provided actionable recommendations to fix their usability issues, and worked with the CRO team at RedEye to discuss future AB tests.

I also wireframed their new autocentre store pages, which are the first part of the project to go live!
