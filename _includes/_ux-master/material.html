The Programme

Taking your own app idea, we go through each of the topics below. After each section has been explained, you get stuck in, applying what you've just learnt!

1. About UX

Starting from the beginning, we dive into what UX is and what it is not. We discuss how the discipline overlaps with psychology, design, research and development

2. Personas

We create personas, to understand who we're building the app for. Understand their background and demographics, building empathy for our user

3. Empathy Maps

Immerse yourself into the situation of our persona, we create an empathy map. Looking at where our user is on the app, what they're hearing, seeing and feeling at the time

4. User Journeys

Taking a situation from the empathy map, we create a user journey timeline. Here we work out how our persona would achieve one of their goals using our app

5. Prototyping

Moving onto prototyping! We turn our user journey timelines into sketches. Using the POP app we turn the sketches into a prototype on our phones, ready for testing

6. User Testing

At the end of the day we rotate groups. Then you have the opportunity to interview and observe another human use your app idea.
